What is Vindinium?

Vindinium is a turn based online multiplayer game in which four competitors battle to acquire the most land, in the form of mines, and by doing so, get the most gold. Each player has one person, a hero, who they may move around the map. Players must move around to the various gold mines placed around the map, and try and take them over. These mines are protected by goblins, and once the goblin is defeated, the mine is acquired. When a mine is owned, the owner receives one gold per round from each mine that they hold.
Additionally, players have the ability to fight against one another, once a single turn is finished. If one hero kills another, and the player who was killed had control of a number of mines, then the killer takes over the previously owned mines. Furthermore, if a player wants to regain health which may have been lost during battle, they may pay a visit to the tavern. At the tavern, they will be able to buy a drink for the cost of 2 gold. The drink will restore 50 health, at most, although they can purchase several drinks at a time. The player who was killed will return to their spawn point to respawn immediately the game ceases after 1200 total turns, and the winneris decided based upon the final amount of gold that players have.

A main goal of the creators of Vindinium was to have people develop unique bots to fight agianst one another. They did this by giving people certain specifications and guidlines that they needed to follow, and allowing them to design their own bots to fight with. This aspect creates really fierce competition. Along with the elo system that works to rank players, Vindinium is a game which induces creativity and a competitive nature.
