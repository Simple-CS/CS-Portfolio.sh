About Me!

Hello! My name is Ethan Edelhart, and I am a student in
Mr. Hesby's Introduction to Computer Science Class.

I am a person who loves to travel, as I have been to the countries

among others. I also enjoy the activities of completing mathematics problems,
watching CS:GO, and observing/learning poker.

One of my favourite things in the world is Twitch.tv. This website,
for those of you who don't know, is one in which users live stream
themselves playing video games or talking about some type of game
for viewers over the internet to watch/listen to. The appeal of this site,
in my opinion, is the ability to be entertained while learning about
nearly any game at any time of day, from people all over the world.
I came across the webstie while looking around YouTube.com, seeing
that some content creators were live streaming their videos. From that
point forward, Twitch became one of the most essential websites I have
ever had the pleasure of coming across. On a different note, one of my
favourite foods is teriyaki chicken with rice, for the reason that it combines
my favourite type of meat with my favourite side dish. When both foods
come together, it makes for an all around perfect meal.

One hobby which I enjoy partaking in is playing piano. I consider this
to be a hobby of mine becase it is something that I enjoy to do on a
regular basis, and it also began my love for music as a whole. I have
